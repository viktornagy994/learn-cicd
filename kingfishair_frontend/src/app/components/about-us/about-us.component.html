About us

The Airline

Where Excellence Takes Flight. Find out why our passengers keep flying with us.

Every day, we do our very best to be your favourite airline. With a strong commitment to customer satisfaction, passion for travel yet zero negative impact on the environment, KingfishAir offers exceptional service and seamless journeys.

From friendly smiles to state-of-the-art aircraft, we connect the North, ensuring safe, comfortable and unforgettable experiences whilst protecting our planet. As we are committed to save the environment, we operate the most modern, hydrogen-powered, zero emission aircrafts designed and manufactured by exceptionally talented engineers and scientists. Together with the experience and forward-thinking perspective of our long-term partner, Airbus, we are making our way to a 100% sustainable, carbon-neutral aviation industry.
