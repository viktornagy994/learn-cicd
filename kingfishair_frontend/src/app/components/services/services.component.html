Checked-in Baggage

What is a Carry-on Bag? A carry- on bag is the type of luggage travelers are allowed to take into an airplane. These baggages are transported in the cargo area of the plane. Checking luggage means you do not have to carry it through the airport. If you have more to pack than will fit in a carry-on, it is best to chose this option!
